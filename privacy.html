The short version: we collect as little data as possible. Most of our apps work entirely on your device — no account, no tracking, no ads. This policy explains how information is handled across all of our apps and services.

01Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications, desktop applications, websites, and related services (collectively, the “Services”) published by Sufyan Rafiq (“we”, “us”, or “our”). It covers every app we publish today and any app we release in the future.

We build software with a simple principle: collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use our Services, how it is used, and the choices and rights you have. By using any of our Services, you agree to the practices described here.

02Information We Collect

Most of our apps are designed to work entirely on your device. They do not require an account, sign-up, or login, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifier just to use them. Depending on the app and the features you choose to use, the following limited categories of information may be processed:

  • Information you create in an app — content you enter, such as entries, preferences, goals, and settings. This is stored locally on your device, unless an app clearly offers an optional sync feature (for example, iCloud), in which case syncing is handled by your platform account.
  • Support communications — if you contact us by email, we receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use them only to respond to you.
  • Diagnostics — if you have opted in to share diagnostics with developers in your device settings, the platform (for example, Apple) may provide us with anonymized crash reports and aggregated statistics. These do not identify you personally.

We do not sell personal information, we do not display third-party advertising, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

03How Information Is Used

Any information described above is used only to:

  • provide and operate the features of our Services;
  • respond to your support requests and communicate with you when you contact us;
  • maintain and improve the stability, performance, and quality of our Services;
  • process purchases through the platform’s payment system; and
  • comply with applicable legal obligations.

We never use your information for third-party advertising, profiling, or resale.

04Permissions

Some features may request system permissions — for example, notifications, camera, photo library, microphone, location, or health data. Permissions are requested only when a feature you choose to use needs them, and the app explains why at the point of the request.

You can decline any permission, and you can change or revoke permissions at any time in your device’s system settings. Declining a permission only affects the feature that depends on it; the rest of the app remains fully usable.

05Analytics

Our apps do not include third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking technologies. Where analytics are used at all, we rely on privacy-respecting, aggregated, and anonymized data — primarily the opt-in analytics that Apple and other platforms provide to developers (such as download counts and anonymized crash statistics). This data cannot be used to identify you.

06Purchases & Subscriptions

Some of our apps offer optional one-time purchases or subscriptions. All payments are processed by the platform’s official payment system — for example, Apple’s In-App Purchase system. We never see, receive, or store your payment card details.

Purchase records are associated with your platform account (for example, your Apple Account) and are governed by the platform’s own privacy policy, such as Apple’s Privacy Policy. You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time in your platform account settings.

07Health Data

This section applies only to apps that offer health and fitness features (for example, integration with Apple Health / HealthKit). Where such features exist:

  • health data is accessed only with your explicit permission;
  • it is used solely to provide the feature you enabled;
  • it is never used for advertising or marketing, never sold, and never shared with third parties;
  • you can revoke access at any time in your device’s health or privacy settings.

Apps that do not offer health features do not access health data at all.

08Third-Party Services

Our Services are built primarily on platform frameworks provided by Apple and other operating system vendors (for example, iCloud sync, notifications, and in-app purchases). When these platform services process data, they do so under the platform’s own privacy policy.

If a specific app integrates an additional third-party service, that integration is disclosed on the app’s App Store page and, where appropriate, inside the app itself. We choose third-party services carefully and prefer those that minimize data collection.

09Data Retention

Data stored on your device remains there until you delete it — either within the app (where an erase or reset option is provided) or by uninstalling the app. We do not keep server-side copies of your app content.

Support emails are retained only as long as needed to help you and to keep a reasonable record of the conversation, after which they may be deleted.

10Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. Because our apps store data on your device, it is protected by your operating system’s built-in security — including sandboxing and device encryption.

No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but our approach of collecting as little data as possible means there is very little that could ever be exposed.

11Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because our apps generally collect no personal information at all, this applies to users of every age.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12International Users

Our Services are available worldwide. Because our apps are designed to keep data on your device, your information generally does not leave your device or your platform account’s storage region.

Where limited information does reach us — for example, a support email — we handle it in accordance with this policy and with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR for users in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom.

13Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Because our apps keep data on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly: view and edit your data in the app, export it where an export feature is provided, or erase it by using the app’s reset option or uninstalling the app. For anything else — including requests about support correspondence — contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly.

14Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time — for example, when we release new apps or features. Updates are published on this page and the “Last updated” date above is revised. For material changes, we will provide additional notice within the affected Services where appropriate.
